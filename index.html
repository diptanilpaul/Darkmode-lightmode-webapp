Develop Your Skills

To develop your skills online, first decide what you want to learn, like web development, data analytics, or cybersecurity. Then, use online platforms like Coursera, Udemy, or freeCodeCamp to take courses and watch tutorials. The key is to practice consistently by doing small projects or exercises while you learn. As you gain experience, showcase your work on GitHub or a personal portfolio. Finally, completing courses and earning certificates can help validate your skills to employers.
